Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right robot for my needs?

Our team of experts will work with you to understand your specific requirements, constraints, and goals. We'll analyze your processes and recommend the most suitable robotic solutions from our wide range of options. You can schedule a consultation through our contact form or call us directly.

Do you offer maintenance services for your robots?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance packages for all our robots. These include regular check-ups, software updates, and technical support. We also offer emergency repair services to minimize downtime. Our maintenance contracts can be customized to suit your operational needs and budget.

Can I request a demo before making a purchase?

Absolutely! We encourage potential clients to see our robots in action before making a decision. We offer both on-site demos at your facility and virtual demonstrations. To schedule a demo, please fill out our contact form specifying the type of robot you're interested in, and our team will get back to you within 24 hours.

What industries do you specialize in?

Our robotics solutions cater to a wide range of industries, including manufacturing, construction and renovation, healthcare, logistics, agriculture, and domestic applications. Our expertise spans both industrial automation and consumer-grade robotic solutions. If you're unsure whether we serve your specific industry, please contact us with details about your business needs.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer several financing options to help make our robotic solutions more accessible, including leasing, rental plans, and payment installments. We also work with several financial partners who specialize in technology investments. Our sales team can provide you with a detailed overview of the available financing options based on your specific requirements.
